FAQ

Common questions

What's the difference between the three plans?
Template Only ($1,299 one-time) — You get the full source code and set it up yourself using our detailed guide. Best for developers or tech-savvy owners who want full control and zero ongoing fees.

Guided Setup ($2,499 one-time) — We set everything up together across 3 live Zoom sessions. You walk away owning the site on your own accounts, with 90 days of email support and 12 months of updates included. No monthly fees ever.

Done For You ($349/month) — We handle everything: deployment, configuration, hosting oversight, updates, and ongoing support. You just fill out a short intake form and your site is live in under 48 hours.
What does $349/month cover for Done For You?
Everything: your Netlify hosting, Supabase database, initial deployment and configuration of all integrations (Stripe, email/SMS notifications), ongoing maintenance, all code updates, and priority email support. The template license is included — you don't pay anything extra upfront. Third-party service costs (Twilio, Stripe transaction fees, your domain) are paid directly by your practice and not included.
What happens if I cancel the Done For You plan?
You can cancel anytime — there's no long-term contract. If you cancel within your first 12 months and want to keep your site running on your own hosting, a one-time $1,299 template license fee applies. After 12 months, you can take full ownership of your site files at no additional charge.
Can I upgrade from Template Only to Done For You later?
Yes. If you buy the Template Only license and later decide you'd prefer managed hosting, we can transition you to the Done For You plan. We'll credit the $1,299 you paid toward your first few months. Contact us to discuss.
What is the Annual Update Subscription for?
Template Only and Guided Setup buyers own the code as it was at the time of purchase. The $249/year Update Subscription gives you access to all new features, security patches, and improvements we release throughout the year — delivered as updated source code. Done For You subscribers receive all updates automatically at no extra cost.
Can I customize the design?
Yes — the admin panel lets you set your logo, brand colors, fonts, services, team bios, hours, and all page content without touching code. For deeper custom work (new layouts or features beyond the template), we offer custom development as an add-on — contact us for a quote.
What third-party accounts will I need?
All plans require a Supabase account (free tier works; $25/mo Pro adds a HIPAA BAA) and a Netlify account (free tier). For payments: a Stripe account (free to create, 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction). For SMS: a Twilio account (optional, ~$0.01/text). For calendar sync: a Google account. For Done For You, we handle the technical setup on your accounts — you just create them.
Is patient data secure and HIPAA-compliant?
Patient data is stored in Supabase with row-level security — only authenticated users can access their own records. For full HIPAA compliance, Supabase Pro ($25/mo) with a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is required. Our setup guide covers this step-by-step. We recommend consulting a healthcare compliance professional for your specific situation.
Is there a live demo I can try?
Yes — demo.wellspringweb.com is a fully working demo. You can go through the booking flow, log in to the patient portal, and access the admin panel to see how customization works — before buying anything.
