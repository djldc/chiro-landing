FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to know how to code?
No. The admin panel lets you change your logo, colors, services, hours, team bios, and all content without touching any code. For setup, you'll need accounts on Netlify (free) and Supabase (free) — both are beginner-friendly and fully documented in the included setup guide.
What are the ongoing costs after I buy?
For most chiropractic practices, ongoing costs are $0/month. Netlify's free tier handles your hosting, Supabase's free tier handles your database and patient logins, and Resend's free tier covers up to 3,000 emails/month. You only pay if your traffic or email volume grows significantly.
How long does setup take?
The built-in Launch Wizard walks you through practice name, contact info, brand colors, hours, and team — typically 10–15 minutes. Deploying to Netlify takes another 5 minutes. Full setup including integrations (Google Calendar, email, etc.) takes about an hour.
Can I customize the design?
Yes — extensively. The admin panel controls colors, fonts, logo, layout sections, and content. Since you receive the full source code (React), a developer can customize anything further. There are no locked-down templates or proprietary platforms.
What does "single site license" mean?
You can deploy ChiroTemplate to one domain (e.g., your practice website). The license key is tied to your site's domain. If you're a web developer building sites for multiple clients, please contact us about agency/multi-site licensing.
Is there a demo I can try?
Yes — demo.wellspringweb.com is a fully working demo. You can explore the public site, go through the booking flow, and see the patient portal. The admin panel is also accessible in the demo.
What if I need help after purchase?
Every purchase includes a detailed setup guide covering all features and integrations. For additional support, WellSpring Digital offers setup and customization services — contact us.
